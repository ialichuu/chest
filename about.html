About Me

I'm Alexandria, but my friends call me Alex. I’m a UI/UX designer with a background in Cognitive Science, digital marketing, graphic design, and a drive to learn and help impact to people’s lives. I'm currently taking a 9-month bootcamp to solidify my UI/UX design skills. I'm also on the hunt for a UX Designer position, thriving in an environment where I can design meaningful experiences for people alongside my peers.

My journey into UX began when I realized that there were more ways of helping people other than becoming a doctor. I found my calling in Cognitive Science at The University of Texas at Dallas. I found comfort in a field where I could help meet the needs of people by studying the mind and understanding its structure. For me, my B.S. in Cognitive Science was the perfect mix of psychology, design, and human-computer interaction.

Outside of my interest in UI/UX Design, I'm an avid consumer of boba tea. If you have any drink recommendations, please let me know! I also like drawing, playing online games like Overwatch and Minecraft, working out, listening to news and tech podcasts, and talking about skincare and makeup.
