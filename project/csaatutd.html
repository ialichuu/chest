CSA At UTD

A redesign of the website for the Chemistry Student Association at UT Dallas

Overview

The Chemistry Student Association (CSA) is a club at The University of Texas at Dallas. They hosted their original website on Wix which has been outdated since 2017.

The Challenge

How do we create a streamlined experience for UTD students interested in joining CSA at UTD?

My Role

This project was done for CSA at UTD. I am not affiliated with this club. This project was done on weekends from November 2019 - April 2020.

Methods/Tools Used

Adobe XD | Wireframing | Interviewing | Low/Mid-Fidelity Design | Hi-Fidelity Prototyping

Officer Interviews

To determine what could be improved with the current website, I asked the officers what their main concerns for the redesign were. I was able to come up with three main ones:

  • Current CSA members view the Facebook page to keep up to date with events. The website is tailored for students who want to become members.
  • CSA sells lab equipment like goggles and carbon copy notebooks during the beginning of the year at a booth. However, students don't know that CSA still sells materials throughout the year and would like to indicate that on their site.
  • Events are generally posted on Google Calendars.

Overall, the team wanted to create a pleasant experience for UTD students who are interested in the Chemistry Student Association at The University of Texas at Dallas to go onto the page to learn more about the benefits for the club as well as upcoming events.

Visual Design

I used a simple monochromatic color palette, focusing on the color blue while adding orange to accent it. I chose this color palette because it was similar to the American Chemistry Society logo and website. However, the scheme is primarily light to give it a light-hearted and non-serious vibe to it because the goal of the Chemistry Student Association is to have fun doing chemistry, rather than the feeling of learning in a bleak lecture hall. I sprinkled in some simple chemistry-related icons I created in Adobe XD to add to the fun vibe.
