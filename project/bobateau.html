BobaTeaU

Helping local boba addicts find their favorite boba shops & drinks near campus

Problem

It's finals season.

You know what that means.

It's time to hone in on your testing skills and find the perfect place to study with your best-tea (or your bo-bae).

Now all puns aside, if you go to The University of Texas at Dallas or have ever been in the Dallas area, you've probably seen at least one or two boba tea shops. From Kung Fu Tea, bobaLand, Boba Latte, and many, many others, the boba tea business is booming around here.

I, a self-proclaimed boba connoisseur, and many other of my fellow boba connoisseurs, cannot try out all of these shops while remembering what drinks I like at all of them.

This project was done for my Internet Studio course at UTD. My professor, Ken Starzer, provided feedback along the way.

Goal

Help students save money by finding their favorite drinks and perhaps even a soul-boba-tea-mate.

Role

UX Designer & Web Developer
